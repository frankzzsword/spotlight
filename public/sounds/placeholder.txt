This directory contains sound effects for the survey application.

The app needs the following sound files:
1. start-sound.mp3 - Played when the survey starts
2. page-change.mp3 - Played when navigating between pages
3. completion-sound.mp3 - Played when the survey is completed

You can add your own sound files with these names or use free sound effects from sites like:
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://freesound.org/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/
